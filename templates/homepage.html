Stock Balancing

About

Welcome to the Stock Balancing website made specially for the ABinBev's Maverick 2.0 Hackathon. Here is a quick guide on how to use the website. Currently, you are on the homepage. You can see the three options below the heading namely Grid wise, Path wise and Product wise.

Grid Wise -
With the Grid Wise specification, you can choose a particular grid, that is a particular pair of product and brewery, you can get the required datafields like Amount to be transferred, Initial CS/ROP ratio and final CS/ROP ratio.

Path Wise -
With the Path Wise specification, you can choose a particular starting location and a destination for the transfer of products. This will give you the Product SKU and the amount of it to be transferred.

Product Wise -
With the Product Wise specification, you can choose a particular Product SKU, this will give you all the paths for the product that needs to be transferred and the amount associated with it.
